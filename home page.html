About Comic Vault

Founded in 1995, Comic Vault began as a small neighborhood comic shop and has grown into one of the largest comic repositories on the East Coast. We specialize in preserving comics from the Golden Age to modern releases, offering lending services, rare comic auctions, and monthly community events. Our mission is to keep the art of comic storytelling alive while making it accessible to everyone.
